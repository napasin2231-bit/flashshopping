Product demo

Creator workflow from product import to social publishing.

This page explains the end-to-end workflow reviewers can inspect without logging in.

Step 1

Import product data

The creator uploads product CSV data containing item names, prices, commission rates, and product links.

Step 2

Prepare media assets

The app organizes item folders and prepares product images, preview images, and a vertical video file for each product.

Step 3

Generate caption

The caption includes a concise product message, price, call to action, and affiliate link selected by the user.

Step 4

Connect TikTok account

The user connects TikTok through OAuth. The app does not ask for or store TikTok passwords.

Step 5

Review before publishing

The user can verify the video, caption, selected account, and posting destination before submitting.

Step 6

Track publishing results

Each publish attempt is recorded with platform, status, timestamp, and message for troubleshooting.
