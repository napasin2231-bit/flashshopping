Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Flash Shopping Manager helps creators manage product content and social publishing workflows. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, and connected platform account identifiers.
  • Product and content data uploaded by the user, including item names, prices, product links, captions, images, and videos.
  • OAuth tokens or refresh tokens required to connect supported platforms, when authorized by the user.
  • Publishing logs such as platform, timestamp, status, and error messages.

How we use information

  • To prepare product videos, captions, and publishing queues.
  • To publish content only when initiated or scheduled by the authorized user.
  • To maintain account connection status and token expiry warnings.
  • To troubleshoot failed publishing attempts and improve reliability.

TikTok data

When a user connects TikTok, we use TikTok OAuth tokens only to perform authorized actions such as uploading or publishing user-selected videos through approved TikTok APIs. We do not collect TikTok passwords and do not sell TikTok user data.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with connected services as required to perform user-authorized publishing actions, or when required by law.

Data retention

We keep account, content, token, and publishing log data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with obligations, or resolve support issues.

User control

Users may request deletion of their account data, uploaded content, and connected account tokens by contacting support.
